My story

I graduated from the University of Kentucky in 2003 with a B.S. in Plant and Soil Science. I spent over a decade working in golf course management, where I honed my skills in team leadership, problem-solving, and project management. In 2016, I transitioned to the lawn care industry, taking on the role of General Manager at Go Green Lawn Solutions. Here, I combined my passion for technology with my expertise in lawn care to develop innovative solutions that improve efficiency and service quality. I am looking to transition into a full-time web devloper role, where I can leverage my unique background and skills to create impactful web applications.

Work Experience

I started out of college working at Valhalla Golf Club in Louisville, KY in 2004. I learned the ins and outs of being a professional groundskeeper for a top-notch golf facility. We hosted numerous major championships, including:

  • 2004 and 2011 Senior PGA
  • 2008 Ryder Cup
  • 2014 PGA Championship
I ran the crew of 20-30 people, assigning daily tasks, managing the irrigatioin system, making sure our chemicals were being applied when needed, and maintaining superb playing conditions.


After leaving Valhalla, I joined my former colleagues at Go Green Lawn Solutions as a lawn care technician. I am still there today and have helped grow the company by providing outstaning service and quality to people's lawns for over 10 years now. I felt like I wanted to do more with my life and wanted to find a possible career change where I could make more money to help pay for my kids education,especially as high-school and college approach. I somehow came across freeCodeCamp.org and starting teaching myself while I waited for over a year to get into the Code:You program. I'm very excited about what I'm learning and can't wait to put it to more use one day! I was into art as a younger kid and I compare designing websites to a modern art and find it faschinating!
